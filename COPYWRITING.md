# SafeFlow Thailand — Copywriting Guide

## Brand voice

Use language that feels:
- personal
- calm
- smart
- warm
- travel-native
- not robotic

---

## Taxi checker copy

Title:
```text
Check if this ride looks fair
```

Subtitle:
```text
Estimate a fair range and spot suspicious signals before you accept.
```

Risk explanation:
```text
This fare looks unusual compared with the mock fair range for this route.
```

Action:
```text
Ask for the meter, use an official queue, or compare with a ride-hailing estimate.
```

## Risk wording

Use:
- “Reroute recommended”
- “Go with awareness”
- “This may feel uncomfortable right now”
- “This looks unusual”
- “Better option nearby”

Avoid:
- “Dangerous”
- “Guaranteed”
- “Definitely scam”
- “Unsafe area”
